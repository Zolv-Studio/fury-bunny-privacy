Privacy Policy — Fury Bunny

Developer: Zolv Studio
App: Fury Bunny (com.zolv.studio.furybunny)
Last updated: July 10, 2026
Contact: contact@zolv.studio

Zolv Studio ("we", "us") operates the mobile game Fury Bunny. This Privacy Policy explains what information is handled when you use the app.

1. Summary

  • We do not require an account or collect your name, email, or phone number.
  • Game progress is stored locally on your device only.
  • We do not sell your personal data.
  • The current version does not display advertisements.

2. Information stored on your device

Fury Bunny saves gameplay data on your device using local storage, including:

  • carrot count and upgrades
  • unlocked skins and selected skin
  • achievements
  • music and sound effect settings (on/off)

This data stays on your device. We do not receive it on our servers. If you uninstall the app or clear app data, this information may be lost.

3. Internet connection

Fury Bunny may connect to the internet to load fonts from Google Fonts. When fonts are loaded, Google may process technical data such as your IP address according to its own privacy policy: Google Privacy Policy.

Apart from font loading, the app does not send your gameplay data to us or to third parties.

4. Permissions

The Android app requests:

  • Internet — used to load Google Fonts (see section 3).

The app may keep the screen awake during gameplay using the device screen wake lock API.

5. Advertising

The current version of Fury Bunny does not show ads and does not use advertising identifiers.

A future update may add optional rewarded ads (for example, to earn in-game carrots). If that happens, this policy will be updated and the Google Play listing will be updated accordingly.

6. Children's privacy

Fury Bunny is a casual arcade game. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Because we do not collect personal information, no parental consent is required for data collection by us.

7. Data retention and security

Game data is stored only on your device. We do not operate servers that store your gameplay progress. While no method of electronic storage is 100% secure, we designed the app to minimize data handling.

8. Your choices

  • You can turn off music and sound effects in the in-game Settings.
  • You can delete local data by clearing the app's storage or uninstalling the app.
  • You can use the device without internet; fonts may fall back to system fonts.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will be revised. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.
